Municipal Corporation
of Bathinda - 2003

The Bathinda Municipal Corporation is a nagar nigam (municipal corporation) which administers the city of Bathinda, Punjab.

It has 50 members elected with a first-past-the-post voting system and 1 ex-officio member which is MLA for Bathinda Urban.
